Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't finish the book?

That is completely fine. Nearly 30% of adults struggle to finish what they start reading. Our cohorts are designed around the social experience. You will never be judged or tested on your reading completion rate.

Why does this cost €60-€120 when other clubs are free?

Free groups suffer from high drop-out rates and unfocused time. Your fee acts as a commitment device to ensure a dedicated group, provides a trained librarian to expertly moderate the discussion, and covers your post-meeting hospitality.

I struggle to focus for more than a few minutes. Is this for me?

Yes. We know 35% of adults are "lapsed readers" battling digital distractions. You can take our quiz below to be matched into our "Slow Read" stream, designed specifically for your pacing.

How do I know I won't get stuck in a group with a mismatched vibe?

We use a strict matchmaking qualifier. You will only be placed in a cohort with people who share your exact reading habits, attention span, and social preferences.
